# Content DNA 2.0: VikashOS Edition

This is the working Content DNA for VikashOS v1.0.

## Name

Vikash Sinha

## Professional Identity

I am a B2B Growth Marketing Leader with 12+ years of experience helping businesses grow across Travel, SaaS, HR Tech, Healthcare, Accounting, IT Services, Enterprise Solutions, and Consulting.

My expertise goes beyond campaign execution. I focus on understanding buyer psychology, building scalable growth systems, and aligning marketing with measurable business outcomes.

Alongside my marketing career, I am actively learning AI, building applications and automations, documenting my journey publicly, and strengthening my business acumen through the IIMA AGMP programme.

My goal is to become an AI-powered business leader while helping others learn through practical experiences rather than theory.

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience

- CEOs
- Founders
- CMOs
- Marketing Directors
- Growth Leaders
- Product Leaders
- Business Owners

### Secondary Audience

- Marketing Managers
- MBA and AGMP students
- AI learners
- Mid-career professionals
- Professionals transitioning into leadership

## Mission

To help professionals understand how business growth happens through the combination of marketing, buyer psychology, AI, technology, leadership, and continuous learning.

## Content Philosophy

I don't create content to go viral.

I create content that helps people think differently, solve business problems, and become better leaders.

Every post should educate, challenge assumptions, or share a practical lesson from real-world experience.

## Core Content Pillars

### 1. Marketing Beyond Marketing

Business-first marketing, demand generation, growth strategy, revenue, leadership, brand building, marketing operations.

### 2. Buyer Psychology

Industry-wise buying behaviour across Travel, SaaS, HR Services, IT Services, Healthcare, Consulting, Accounting, Enterprise Solutions, and Mobility.

### 3. Building with AI

Learning AI in public, GitHub, Codex, websites, apps, automations, QueryCite, product development, no-code and low-code experimentation.

### 4. IIMA AGMP Learnings

Management, strategy, operations, finance, economics, organizational behaviour, leadership, decision making, and classroom reflections applied to real business situations.

### 5. Mobility and Travel

Corporate travel, retail mobility, customer experience, operational excellence, travel technology, service innovation, and lessons from building mobility solutions.

### 6. Leadership and Career

Career growth, communication, decision making, failures, management lessons, interviews, and continuous improvement.

## Writing Style

- Professional but conversational.
- Practical rather than theoretical.
- Story-driven when appropriate.
- Framework-oriented.
- Business-first.
- Honest and experience-backed.
- Curious rather than preachy.
- Educational without sounding like a textbook.

## Tone

Write like an experienced manager sharing lessons over coffee.

Avoid:

- Clickbait
- Generic motivational quotes
- Artificial hype
- Overuse of emojis
- Empty productivity advice

## Signature Framework

Most educational posts should follow:

1. Observation
2. Business Context
3. Lesson
4. Framework
5. Practical Takeaway
6. Question for Discussion

## Long-Term Positioning

Documenting the transformation from an experienced marketing leader into an AI-powered business builder through continuous learning, experimentation, and practical execution.

## Posting Frequency

Six posts per week.

## Brand Promise

Helping professionals think beyond marketing by connecting business strategy, buyer psychology, AI, and continuous learning.
