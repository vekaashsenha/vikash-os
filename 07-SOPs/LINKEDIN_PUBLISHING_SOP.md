# LinkedIn Publishing SOP

Use this process before publishing any VikashOS LinkedIn post.

## 1. Choose The Series

Confirm the post belongs to one of the six permanent series:

- Marketing Beyond Marketing
- Buyer Psychology With Vikash
- Build With Vikash
- AGMP With Vikash
- Mobility With Vikash
- Leadership With Vikash

## 2. Validate The Idea

The post should have:

- A clear observation.
- A business or learning context.
- A practical lesson.
- A natural human voice.
- No invented story or unsupported number.

## 3. Draft The Post

Preferred structure for educational posts:

1. Observation
2. Business Context
3. Lesson
4. Framework
5. Practical Takeaway
6. Question for Discussion

Use the structure only when it helps. Do not force it into personal stories or build logs.

## 4. Human Voice Review

Remove:

- Clickbait.
- Artificial hype.
- Generic AI phrases.
- Empty motivation.
- Excessive emojis.
- Influencer-style exaggeration.

Improve:

- Specificity.
- Clarity.
- Practical value.
- Natural sentence flow.

## 5. Evidence Check

Before publishing, confirm:

- Any numbers are real and allowed to share.
- Any personal story is accurate.
- Any company or client detail is safe to mention.
- Any research claim has a source.

## 6. Publishing Details

Add:

- Series hashtag.
- Publish date.
- Optional supporting visual.
- Follow-up question.

## 7. After Publishing

Within 24 hours:

- Reply to meaningful comments.
- Save useful responses.
- Capture follow-up ideas in the Knowledge Vault.
- Note what worked in the weekly review.
