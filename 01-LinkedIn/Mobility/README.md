# Mobility With Vikash

Hashtag: `#MobilityWithVikash`

This series explores corporate travel, retail mobility, customer experience, operational excellence, travel technology, service innovation, and lessons from building mobility solutions.

The goal is to make mobility and travel thinking more useful for business leaders, marketers, and operators.

## Useful Angles

- Why mobility is both an operations problem and a customer experience problem.
- How corporate travel buyers evaluate service, cost, reliability, and control.
- What travel brands can learn from B2B buying behaviour.
- How technology can reduce friction without removing human accountability.
- Why service recovery matters in travel and mobility.

## Draft Standard

Use specific observations. Avoid broad claims about the travel industry unless they can be supported.
