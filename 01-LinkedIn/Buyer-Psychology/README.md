# Buyer Psychology With Vikash

Hashtag: `#BuyerPsychologyWithVikash`

This series studies how people and buying committees make business decisions across industries.

The goal is to move beyond surface-level marketing and understand what different stakeholders care about, fear, compare, question, and approve.

## Core Questions

- Who is involved in the buying decision?
- What does each stakeholder care about?
- What triggers the need?
- What creates hesitation?
- What criteria shape evaluation?
- What should marketing communicate?
- What should sales avoid?

## Working Rule

Do not invent buyer behaviour. Use real observations, industry knowledge, sales conversations, customer feedback, public research, and clearly marked assumptions.
