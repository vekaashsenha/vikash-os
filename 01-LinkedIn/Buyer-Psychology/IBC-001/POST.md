# Inside the Buying Committee #001

Corporate travel may look like one purchase.

Inside the company, it is several decisions happening at once.

Finance is looking for control, savings, and visibility over spend.

HR is thinking about employee experience and safety.

Procurement is weighing compliance and commercial value.

Travellers want convenience.

Leadership wants visibility and business continuity.

Same travel programme.

Different definitions of value.

This is what makes B2B buying more complex than it first appears.

A message that works for one stakeholder may leave another unconvinced.

Talking only about savings may miss the experience question.

Talking only about convenience may miss the control question.

Talking only about policy may miss what happens when an important journey is disrupted.

The real job is not to find one benefit and repeat it to everyone.

It is to understand how the same decision must make sense to every person who can influence it.

In a buying committee, the product stays the same.

The reason to say yes changes from seat to seat.
