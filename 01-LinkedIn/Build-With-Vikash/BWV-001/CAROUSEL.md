# Carousel: Ideas Don't Build Products

## Format

- 7 slides
- One clear idea per slide
- Concise, mobile-readable copy
- Human, professional, and restrained tone

## Slide 1 — Hook

**Ideas don't build products.**

**Systems do.**

## Slide 2 — What I Expected

I thought the idea would be the hardest part.

Then I started building.

The idea was actually the easy part.

## Slide 3 — Where the Work Began

Understanding Git.

Managing repositories and workspaces.

Connecting tools.

Writing better prompts and workflows.

## Slide 4 — The Build Cycle

Testing.

Breaking things.

Fixing them.

Then breaking something else.

## Slide 5 — Why Systems Matter

A small mistake in one part can stop everything.

One permission.  
One missing folder.  
One broken integration.

## Slide 6 — The Marketing Parallel

A campaign is never just an advertisement.

It is a system.

Software is not much different.

## Slide 7 — The Question

Every mistake leaves behind a better system than the one I had yesterday.

What's one thing you thought would be the hardest part of building, only to discover the real challenge was somewhere else?
