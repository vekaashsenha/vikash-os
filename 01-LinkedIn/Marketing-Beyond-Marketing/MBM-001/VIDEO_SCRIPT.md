# Video Script: The 10x ROAS Review

## Format

- First-person, direct-to-camera LinkedIn video
- 45–60 seconds
- Natural, reflective delivery
- No supporting facts beyond the approved post

## Script

I once walked into a performance review feeling confident.

My campaigns were showing ROAS as high as 10x, and I expected that result to speak for itself.

Instead, I was told, “Your ROAS may be 10x, but the business ROI is still negative.”

Then came the harder feedback: I understood marketing metrics, but I was not yet ready to discuss business ROI.

It hurt. But it forced me to look beyond the campaign dashboard.

ROAS showed revenue compared with ad spend. It did not tell me whether the business was actually making money.

For that, I needed to understand pricing, margins, sales conversion, operations, retention, cash flow, and the complete business process.

Marketing performance is not automatically business performance.

Before celebrating campaign performance, ask whether it is creating profitable business growth.

## On-Screen Text

Use no more than three text moments:

1. `ROAS: as high as 10x`
2. `Business ROI: negative`
3. `Marketing performance ≠ business performance`

## Delivery Notes

- Speak as if sharing a considered professional reflection.
- Pause after the sentence about negative business ROI.
- Keep the pace steady and conversational.
- Avoid dramatic music, exaggerated reactions, and rapid transitions.
- Do not add a company, date, campaign, spend figure, revenue figure, or business result.
