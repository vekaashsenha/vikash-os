# Carousel: Marketing Performance Is Not Business Performance

## Format

- 7 slides
- One clear idea per slide
- Concise copy with generous spacing
- Professional, restrained visual treatment

## Slide 1 — Hook

**My ROAS was 10x.**  
**The business ROI was still negative.**

## Slide 2 — The Review

I walked into a performance review feeling confident.

My campaigns were showing ROAS as high as 10x.

I expected the result to speak for itself.

## Slide 3 — The Feedback

Instead, I was told:

**“Your ROAS may be 10x, but the business ROI is still negative.”**

## Slide 4 — The Harder Lesson

Then came the harder sentence:

**“You understand marketing metrics, but you are not yet ready to discuss business ROI.”**

## Slide 5 — The Difference

**ROAS** showed the revenue generated compared with ad spend.

**Business ROI** asked a wider question:

Was the business actually making money?

## Slide 6 — Looking Beyond the Campaign

I needed to understand:

Pricing. Margins. Sales conversion. Operations. Retention. Cash flow. The complete business process.

## Slide 7 — The Question

**Marketing performance is not automatically business performance.**

Before celebrating a campaign result, ask whether it is creating profitable business growth.

What difficult feedback changed how you approach your profession?
