# Static Image Brief: The Process Behind the Promise

## Objective

Create one minimal LinkedIn static image that expresses the approved post's central idea: a customer promise depends on the operating process behind it.

## Format

- LinkedIn portrait static image
- 1080 × 1350 px
- Mobile-first readability
- Minimal, text-led composition

## Main Text

**The customer does not experience the advertisement.**

**The customer experiences the process behind it.**

## Supporting Text

Can the operating system honour the promise?

## Series Label

AGMP With Vikash #001

## Visual Hierarchy

1. Lead with the contrast between `advertisement` and `process`.
2. Give the second sentence the strongest typographic emphasis.
3. Keep the supporting question smaller and clearly separate.
4. Place the series label quietly near the lower edge.

## Typography Direction

- Use a refined, highly legible sans-serif typeface.
- Use no more than two type weights.
- Create emphasis through weight, scale, and spacing rather than effects.
- Keep line lengths short and use sentence case.

## Colour and Composition

- Use a restrained neutral palette with one controlled accent colour.
- Maintain generous negative space.
- Use a subtle line or directional structure only if it helps connect promise to process.
- Ensure strong contrast for mobile viewing.

## What to Avoid

- Stock photographs or staged office scenes
- Factory, warehouse, conveyor-belt, or machinery clichés
- Generic dashboards, process diagrams, or fake operational data
- Megaphones, targets, rockets, trophies, or celebration imagery
- Decorative gradients, glossy effects, or excessive shadows
- Additional claims, quotations, statistics, case facts, or classroom details
- Crowded layouts and long explanatory copy

## Suggested Alt Text

Minimal text-led graphic stating that customers do not experience the advertisement; they experience the process behind it, followed by a question about whether the operating system can honour the promise.
