# AGMP With Vikash

Hashtag: `#AGMPWithVikash`

This series turns IIMA AGMP learning into practical business reflections.

The purpose is not to summarize lectures. The purpose is to connect management concepts with real business decisions, marketing leadership, AI adoption, operations, finance, strategy, and people management.

## Topics

- Strategy.
- Operations.
- Finance.
- Economics.
- Organizational behaviour.
- Leadership.
- Decision making.
- Classroom reflections applied to real business situations.

## Draft Standard

Each post should include one learning, one business context, and one practical application.
