## Real Story

Vikash met a founder friend who was worried because sales and website traffic were down.

The founder felt that customers were increasingly searching or asking questions through AI platforms.

The founder also said the business did not have a large budget available.

The conversation made Vikash think about how customer discovery is changing and how smaller businesses need an affordable way to understand whether AI systems can discover, understand, and cite their brand.

That problem connects naturally to why Vikash is building [QueryCite](../../03-Projects/QueryCite/README.md).

## Core Lesson

Businesses have spent years asking how they rank on Google.

They may now also need to ask:

> Can AI systems discover, understand, and trust our business enough to include it in an answer?
