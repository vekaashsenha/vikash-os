# Build With Vikash

Hashtag: `#BuildWithVikash`

This series documents the practical side of learning AI by building.

The focus is not hype. The focus is the real process: choosing a problem, building a small solution, testing it, improving it, and sharing what worked or failed.

## Topics

- GitHub learning.
- Codex workflows.
- Websites and apps.
- Automations.
- QueryCite.
- No-code and low-code experiments.
- AI-assisted product development.

## Draft Standard

Every post should answer:

- What was built or attempted?
- Why does it matter?
- What was difficult?
- What was learned?
- What can someone else apply?
