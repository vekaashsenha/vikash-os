# NPS Widget

This folder is for a lightweight customer feedback and NPS widget idea.

## Problem To Explore

Teams often collect feedback too late or in formats that are difficult to act on. A small, clear feedback widget could help capture sentiment closer to the customer experience.

## Early Direction

- Simple feedback capture.
- Clear NPS score flow.
- Optional qualitative comment.
- Dashboard or export for review.
- Practical use in service or mobility contexts.
