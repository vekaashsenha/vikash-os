# QueryCite

**Public product name:** QueryCite.com

QueryCite is the existing project record for the product Vikash is building after observing a marketing problem: smaller businesses need an affordable way to understand whether AI systems can discover, understand, and cite their brand.

This file does not claim that the product has proven capabilities, customers, audit results, traffic impact, or business outcomes.

## Problem To Explore

Businesses have spent years asking how they rank on Google. They may increasingly also need to ask whether AI systems can discover, understand, and trust their business enough to include it in an answer.

The size of this shift, its causes, and the right response require evidence. Do not claim that AI has replaced Google or that SEO is dead.

## Product-Building Direction

- Explore how a business can assess its visibility in AI-generated answers.
- Connect visibility findings to understandable evidence and practical next actions.
- Distinguish brand mentions from citations.
- Keep scores explainable rather than presenting an unsupported number.
- Test assumptions, failures, APIs, and output quality before claiming results.
