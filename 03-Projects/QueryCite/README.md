# QueryCite

QueryCite is a project space for exploring better research, source tracking, and citation workflows.

## Problem To Explore

Professionals collect ideas from reports, articles, videos, PDFs, and internal notes, but often lose the source context when turning those ideas into content or business decisions.

## Early Direction

- Capture useful source material.
- Extract key points without losing attribution.
- Turn research into notes, frameworks, and content drafts.
- Keep claims tied to sources.
