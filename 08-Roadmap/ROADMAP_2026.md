# VikashOS Roadmap 2026

## 2026 Direction

Build VikashOS into a practical personal operating system for professional branding, knowledge management, product building, continuous learning, and business growth.

The long-term direction is to document the transformation from experienced B2B marketing leader to AI-powered business builder.

## Main Goals

1. Build a consistent LinkedIn publishing system around six permanent series.
2. Maintain a useful Knowledge Vault that supports content, projects, and career growth.
3. Develop small AI-assisted projects that solve practical problems.
4. Connect IIMA AGMP learning with real business application.
5. Strengthen public positioning around marketing, buyer psychology, AI, mobility, and leadership.
6. Keep the system simple enough to use every week.

## Quarter 1: Foundation

- Set up repository structure.
- Finalize Brand Bible and Content DNA.
- Start weekly publishing rhythm.
- Create first Buyer Psychology draft.
- Capture AGMP and AI learning notes.

## Quarter 2: Consistency

- Publish regularly across all six series.
- Build repeatable LinkedIn drafting and review workflow.
- Add first project logs for QueryCite, NPS Widget, or Document Vault.
- Convert useful notes into frameworks.

## Quarter 3: Productization

- Select one project for deeper development.
- Create public website structure.
- Connect projects with professional positioning.
- Build reusable templates and SOPs from repeated workflows.

## Quarter 4: Review And Scale

- Review what content themes worked best.
- Identify strongest professional positioning.
- Refine resume and website based on proof points.
- Plan the next version of VikashOS.

## Success Measures

- The repository is used weekly.
- Posts feel practical, credible, and human.
- Knowledge notes become reusable assets.
- Projects show visible learning and execution.
- Public positioning becomes sharper over time.

## Reminder

Do not chase scale before usefulness. Build the system, use it honestly, and improve it through real work.
