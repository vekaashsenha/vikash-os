## Rate Definitions

- **Engagement rate:** total available engagements ÷ impressions.
- **Comment rate:** comments ÷ impressions.
- **Save rate:** saves ÷ impressions.

Use the same definitions across periods. If LinkedIn changes available metrics, note the change rather than mixing definitions.
