## Story Bank Rules

- Do not create a second Story Bank in another folder.
- Link to the source rather than copying full approved posts here.
- Do not mark a story `Published` without a confirmed public URL or publishing record.
- Merge overlapping ideas instead of producing repetitive posts.
- Review sensitive stories before any external drafting.
