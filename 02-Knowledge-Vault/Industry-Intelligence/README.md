# Industry Intelligence

This folder contains the Vikash Industry Intelligence Framework, or VIIF.

VIIF is the research system for understanding how industries operate, how buying decisions are made, and how those insights should shape marketing, sales, customer success, and AI opportunities.

## Purpose

Use this folder when researching an industry before creating public content, strategy notes, buyer psychology posts, sales playbooks, or AI opportunity maps.

The public LinkedIn post is only the visible output. The real value comes from the underlying research discipline.

## Important Rule

Broad labels such as "IT" or "Technology" are too generic for serious analysis.

Where relevant, separate them into more useful categories such as:

- IT Services
- Cybersecurity
- Cloud and Computing
- SaaS
- Enterprise Software
- Managed Services
- Technology Consulting
- Infrastructure Services

## Quality Standard

Facts must be sourced. Assumptions must be labelled. Personal experience must not be invented. Recommendations should connect back to research, not preference.
