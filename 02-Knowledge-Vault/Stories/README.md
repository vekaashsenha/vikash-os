# Stories

Use this folder for real experiences that may become posts, talks, examples, or case reflections.

Do not publish from this folder without checking confidentiality, accuracy, and tone.

## Story Capture Rules

- Do not include sensitive client or company information.
- Do not invent details to make a story stronger.
- Capture what happened, what was learned, and what can be shared safely.
- Convert specific incidents into generalized lessons when needed.
